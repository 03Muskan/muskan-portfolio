Donut Store

Donut Store project is a UI/UX design concept for an online donut shop. The focus was on creating a playful, visually appealing, and intuitive interface that guides users smoothly from browsing to choosing their favorite donuts. The design balances aesthetic appeal with usability, ensuring every element serves the user’s needs.

Key Features:

  • Clear Layout & Structure: Grid-based product cards with consistent spacing and alignment for easy scanning.
  • Visually Appealing Design: Soft pastel colors and high-quality donut images to attract attention.
  • Readable Typography: Rounded, friendly fonts for headings; clean fonts for descriptions.
  • Interactive Elements: Hover effects on cards and buttons for visual feedback and engagement.
  • Highlighted Actions: Key buttons like “View Details” and “Order Now” are accent-colored for easy recognition.
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for mobiles, tablets, and desktops ensuring seamless experience.
  • Accessibility Focused: Adequate color contrast, readable font sizes, and clickable elements for all users.
  • Fun & Engaging Experience: Micro-interactions and smooth navigation convey a playful, cheerful brand personality.
Tools & Technologies:
  • Figma: For UI mockups, prototyping, and design iterations.
